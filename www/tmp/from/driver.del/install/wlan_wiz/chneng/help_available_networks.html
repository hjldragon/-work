Available Wireless Networks
If you are in an environment where there are several wireless networks you may see several different wireless networks on this screen. Select the appropriate wireless name and click "Next". If you are not sure which wireless network to select, please speak to your system administrator.

If you do not see your wireless network, click the "Refresh" button to re-discover the wireless networks within your environment. If you are still unable to see any wireless networks and you are sure that the network name (SSID) name is not hidden please check the following:
  1. Make sure that your wireless device is switched on.

  2. Try moving your wireless device closer to the access point or your computer.
If you have configured your access point to hide its SSID name you must manually enter the network name by clicking the "Advanced" button.

Please note: the wireless signal strength is the signal strength between the device and the access point that is communicating with. It is not the wireless signal strength between your computer and the access point.
